Quick overview
Across all Entry, Junior, Experienced and Senior levels, expected salaries after getting an MBA are significantly higher than before. Before the MBA, the Junior level has the highest salary (around $76,000), while the Senior level is the lowest (around $74,500). After the MBA, salaries across all job levels rise to roughly $130,000 – though the Senior level earns just slightly more – highlighting the MBA’s benefits at every career stage.
