From kindergarten, I loved exploring—every storybook and puzzle sparked my curiosity.
Each day was an adventure filled with discovery.
In primary school, my endless questions and enthusiasm grew.
Teachers encouraged my curiosity, helping me carefully approach new challenges and set a foundation for thoughtful learning.
High school taught me balance.
Ambition became purposeful as I took on leadership roles, learning that careful decisions and patience were key to steady growth.
University expanded my world further.
Classes, internships, and projects clarified my goals, showing me that every experience was a learning opportunity.
Entering the workforce deepened my ambition.
I realized growth required continuous learning, leading naturally to my decision to pursue an MBA—a key step toward achieving my future aspirations.

This website is designed to share my journey and the lessons learned along the way. But more than that, it serves as a platform to connect with others who share similar experiences and aspirations to MBA pursuit. Explore the site to learn more about the factors that may influence your decision to pursue an MBA. At the end, there is a section to help you evaluate yourself whether you are ready for an MBA. I hope you find it helpful and inspiring as you navigate your own path toward personal and professional growth.
